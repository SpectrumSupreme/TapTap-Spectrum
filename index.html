GAME RULES

  • The Charge Shot: Hold down Left Click to charge your weapon. The longer you hold it, the bigger the bullet and the more damage it deals!
  • I-Frames: Getting hit by the boss or its math projectiles hurts, but it grants you 1 second of invincibility (flashing) to escape.
  • Phase 2 Earthquake: When the boss drops below 50% HP, watch the floor! If it flashes Orange, get ready to jump. If you are touching the ground when it turns Red, you take massive damage.
  • Data-Driven Engine: If you edit the config.json file, just click the game screen after a "Game Over" to instantly reload the engine with the new stats.

ENGINE SPECS

This game is running natively in the browser using a custom C++ physics engine compiled to WebAssembly via Emscripten.
