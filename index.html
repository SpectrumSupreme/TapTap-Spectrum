Man vs Math: Engine Prototype

C++ SDL2 compiled to WebAssembly.

Controls

  • Movement
    A D
  • Flight / Vertical
    W S
  • Jump SPACE
  • Fire Weapon L-Click

Game Mechanics

  • Charge Shot Hold Left Click to charge. Longer holds equal larger, stronger projectiles.
  • I-Frames Taking damage grants 1 second of invincibility to escape.
  • Phase 2 Earthquake Orange flash = Get ready. Red flash = Jump immediately to avoid damage.
  • JSON Reload Edit config.json and click after Game Over to hot-reload stats.
